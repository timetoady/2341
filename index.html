FLAC testing

Size

34802

Assessment

Like the MP3, the FLAC was the other format that successfully played across all platforms. However, FLAC files seem to have several issues. They were unable to show total play time on any platform from the browser, and starting and stopping playback was sometimes a bit sluggish. And although the time would count up, the position wasn't scrollable on any platform. The quality uptick that should be more noticable for a lossless file, but was less apparent on the Android phone, most on the PC.
